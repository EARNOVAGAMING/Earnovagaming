Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026. We take your privacy seriously. Here is exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.
1. Information We Collect
  • Account Data: Email address, username, and password (hashed) provided at registration.
  • Gameplay Data: Points earned, game scores, session duration, and level progression.
  • Transaction Data: Withdrawal requests, wallet addresses, amounts, and network selections.
  • Referral Data: Who referred you and who you have referred.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies for fraud detection and session management.
2. How We Use Your Data
  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process withdrawal requests and verify your identity.
  • To calculate and credit referral earnings accurately.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and self-referral attempts.
  • To send you account-related emails such as verification, password reset, and withdrawal updates.
  • To improve the platform and fix bugs.
3. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and for fraud detection. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You may decline non-essential cookies via our cookie banner when you first visit. Declining cookies may affect login functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): Our database and authentication provider. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • NOWPayments: Our crypto payment processor for PRO subscriptions. Subject to their Privacy Policy.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law, court order, or to prevent fraud.
5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. Withdrawal records may be retained for 7 years for financial compliance purposes.

6. Your Rights
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at earnova@zohomail.com

7. Security

We use Firebase Authentication with encrypted passwords, HTTPS for all data transmission, and role-based access controls. We conduct regular security reviews. However no system is 100% secure. Please use a strong and unique password for your Earnova account.

8. Children's Privacy

Earnova is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us immediately and we will delete the account.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of Earnova after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us at earnova@zohomail.com
